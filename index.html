College Life

Lot has happened over the first two years of my college life ,When I looks back I see a teenager entering the place where dreams come True,"The IIT". Yes it was a big name back then, it still is ,but much much bigger is the person that this place has shaped me in. Lots of emotions, lots of memories, and don't forget the fun. This place has something in it's air that pushes you harder , challenges you, transforms you into a man of Beliefs, Individuality, Independence, Self-Esteem. The harder you fight with any challenge stronger your belief gets, more powerful you become. If I have to name any 6 of my favourite places of IIT Kanpur in my check list ,that would be.

  • P.K. Kelkar Library
  • Lecture Hall-20
  • Caffe Coffee Day
  • Open Air Theater
  • E-3 / Hall-13
  • Canteen Hall-13,12,4,10
